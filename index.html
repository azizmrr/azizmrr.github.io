About the school

Founded to provide a safe, inclusive and culturally sensitive education space for Rohingya girls, our school offers literacy, numeracy, life skills, psychosocial support and teacher training. We work with families and the local community to ensure sustained attendance and wellbeing.

1
Our mission
Provide safe, quality basic education and pathways to further learning.
2
Our vision
Empowered girls who participate actively in their families and communities.

Programs

Primary education

Literacy, numeracy and foundational science in a gender-sensitive classroom.

Life skills & wellbeing

Health education, hygiene, psychosocial support, and leadership for girls.

Teacher training

Continuous professional development for local female teachers & community facilitators.

Community & family engagement

Parent groups, inclusive decision-making, and community awareness sessions.

Admissions

Enrollment is open for girls aged 6–14. We prioritise vulnerable families. To apply, complete the form below or visit our community office.
